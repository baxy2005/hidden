Utleiras minta

Pick up from the airport and transfer to your chosen accommodation in the Lake Balaton region. Taste freshly caught fish from the lake, organic produce from local farms and herbs from herb garden. Home-made jam, honey, cheese, yoghurt, freshly baked bread and pastries for breakfast as well as traditional clay oven baked dinner for the full authentic experience.

Visit the region's best winemakers, explore unspoiled National Parks and experience colourful traditions of charming little towns and villages during the days or even enjoy a splash in Europe's largest biologically active natural thermal lake, Lake Héviz... Visit a Pálinka Brewery (type of local brandy), a goat cheese or mangalitza farm and picturesque vineyards for the best authentic wine tasting experience.

Visit one of the oldest settlements of the country, have picnic lunch in the open with wonderful views across the lake, work on your sailing skills or just enjoy a boat trip to the nearby historic towns. Try swimming, water sports or simply just relax by the lake. Taste traditional Hungarian dishes, roasted in stone ovens and meals cooked in stew-pots in authentic, thatched roofed restaurants with live gypsy music and folklore programmes. Head back to Budapest or continue your tour to discover other parts of the country...
